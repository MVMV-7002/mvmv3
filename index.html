The Supra MK3(1986-1992)

Introduction: This generation saw a complete redesign of the Supra, with a more aggressive, sporty look and a focus on performance.

Engine: The A60 featured a more powerful 3.0L inline-six (7M-GTE) engine, available in both naturally aspirated and turbocharged versions.
This was a major step forward in performance.

Key Features: The A60 introduced a more aerodynamic design with pop-up headlights, and Toyota incorporated advanced electronics like digital
dashboards and an automatic transmission with overdrive.
